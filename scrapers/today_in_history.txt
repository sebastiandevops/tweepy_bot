1191, Battle of Arsūf On this day in 1191, the Muslim army of Saladin attacked the Crusaders of Richard I (the Lion-Heart) at the Battle of Arsūf, and, though Richard successfully counterattacked in the evening, his march to Jerusalem was delayed.
